Get VAT Return Filing Done By a Certified Tax Adviser

VAT Return should be completed either by a Taxable Person, their legal representative or a Tax Agent and submitted online to the Federal Tax Authority (FTA) as per the schedule assigned by FTA, but no later than the 28th day following the end of the Tax Period (which is either one or three months).

  • Guidelines prior to VAT return submisiion
